Welcome to BetMind

An evidence-based clinical companion designed to help sports bettors manage urges, perform cognitive distraction exercises, and complete behavioral self-assessments.

Step 1

Register Session

Enter any User ID in the left sidebar and click Register. This creates a secure, dedicated session on your local server.

Step 2

Manage Betting Urges

Type your state or click the suggesting chips. The agent will guide you through BREATHE478 (breathing) or DISTRACT5M (grounding) to build your streak.

Step 3

Self-Assessment

Ask the agent to take the PGSI test. Complete the 9-item questionnaire one by one to receive supportive feedback and calculate severity.
